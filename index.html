Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions to know our services and process better. If you don't find the answers you are looking for, feel free to contact us directly.

What services does Rinterio offer?

Rinterio offers a full range of interior design services, including residential and commercial design, space planning, furniture selection, color consultation, and project management. We tailor our services to meet the unique needs and preferences of each client.

How does the design process work at Rinterio?

Our design process begins with an initial consultation where we discuss your vision, needs, and budget. After this, we create a design concept, which includes mood boards, layouts, and material selections. Once you approve the concept, we move on to detailed planning and execution, ensuring every detail is aligned with your expectations.

How long does an interior design project typically take?

The timeline for an interior design project can vary depending on the scope and complexity. On average, residential projects take between 8 to 12 weeks, while larger commercial projects may take longer. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase to keep you informed.

What is the cost of working with Rinterio?

The cost of our services varies based on the size of the project, the scope of work, and the materials selected. We offer a detailed estimate after the initial consultation, ensuring transparency in pricing. We also offer flexible payment plans to accommodate different budgets.

What if I’m not satisfied with the final design?

Our goal is to ensure your complete satisfaction. We involve you throughout the design process to minimize any surprises. If you’re not happy with any aspect of the final design, we’ll work with you to make adjustments until you’re fully satisfied.
